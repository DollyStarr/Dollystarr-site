What is this?

The Dollyhouse™

The Dollyhouse is our interactive PG‑13 membership world. Each month we unlock new Rooms (themes like Space Cowgirl or Strawberry Coquette), drop limited Dolly Cards, and hang out on live shows. Members vote on props, colors and next‑room stories.

Collectibles

Numbered Dolly Cards and mini‑posters keep each Room special. Founder stamps go in your Dollyhouse Passport.

Community

Discord roles by Room, cozy chats, polls and weekly voice calls. Warm, body‑positive and brand‑safe.

Live reveals

Friday tentpoles with outfit reveals, story beats and surprise drops. Catch it live, clip it later.
